We’re excited to unveil our latest collaboration with California brand Free & Easy, a partnership that perfectly captures our shared easy-going attitude. FAQ: Shipping & Returns

1. Can I return my item?
Yes, you can return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Note: “Carver Garage Blem” items are final sale and cannot be returned.

2. Do I have to pay for return shipping?
Yes, unless the return is due to a Carver Skateboards error (e.g., incorrect or defective item). Returns must be sent freight prepaid.

3. When will I get my refund?
Refunds are usually processed within 4 weeks, including transit time (5–10 business days), processing (3–5 business days), and bank processing (5–10 business days).

4. Are there any return fees?
Yes, a 15% re-stocking fee may apply. If using FedEx services and the return is not Carver's fault, you are responsible for shipping costs.

5. How do I start a return?
Email @carverskateboards.com with your order number and product details. They will respond with return instructions.

6. Where do you ship?
Carver only ships to U.S. addresses (no P.O. boxes or international orders). Shipping rates may vary for Alaska and Hawaii.

7. How long does shipping take?
Most orders ship within 72 hours. Delivery time depends on the selected FedEx shipping method (Ground, 2-Day, Overnight, etc.).

8. Can I use a freight forwarder or third-party shipping company?
No. Orders with a “Ship To” address pointing to a freight forwarder will be canceled and refunded. Refunds may take 5–7 business days to process.

9. How are shipping rates calculated?
Shipping costs are weight-based and rounded up to the next full pound.
