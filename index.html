About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Anthony Kim, and I am a student at Arizona State University, majoring in Graphic Information Technology. This website showcases my work and projects.


I'm a web developer with a creative mind and a competitive heart. As a proud member of Club Golf Team at ASU, I’ve learned to balance precision, focus, and teamwork both on the green field and behind the monitor screen.


My journey is a unique blend of athletic discipline and digital creativity. Whether I’m lining up a birdie putt or debugging a responsive layout, I approach every challenge with drive and dedication. Through web development, I’ve found a space where I can build, express, and constantly grow — just like I do in golf.


I believe in the harmony of motion and logic — where athletic energy meets design thinking, and where passion fuels performance. This portfolio showcases not just my technical skills, but the spirit of balance, grit, and curiosity that defines me.


"Code with passion. Swing with purpose."
